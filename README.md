# DSA Graham County Research

Research and data for Democratic Socialists of America chapter formation in Graham County, Arizona (Legislative District 19).

---

## Contents

- **Research Sources**: Demographics, economics, labor data for LD19
- **Website Resources**: Static website for chapter presence
- **Organizing Strategy**: Evidence-based approach for rural Arizona

## Strategic Focus Areas

1. **Economic Populism**: High poverty despite copper wealth
2. **Labor Revival**: Rebuild rank-and-file organizing post-1983 strike
3. **Just Transition**: Workers-first climate policy for mining towns
4. **Healthcare Access**: Rural healthcare deserts, Medicare for All
5. **Housing Justice**: Company town tenant organizing (Morenci)
